Pergolas

These modern, durable outdoor shading systems provide elegant protection for terraces, gardens and hospitality venues. They are available with aluminium or wooden structures, fixed or movable roofs, LED lighting and even side walls.
They are the ideal choice if you want to extend the outdoor season and increase the comfort and value of your home or business.

Advantages of Pergolas

  • Aesthetic, modern appearance in any environment
  • Protection from rain, sun, and wind, usable all year round
  • Movable roof, shading as needed
  • Expandable with side walls, LED lighting, heating
  • Increases the value and comfort of the property
  • Easy to clean, minimal maintenance
