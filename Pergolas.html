Pergolas

Pergolas are modern, durable outdoor shading systems that provide an elegant appearance and protection on terraces, in gardens, or at hospitality venues. Available with aluminum or wooden structures, fixed or movable roofs, LED lighting, and even side walls.
The ideal choice if you want to extend the outdoor season and increase the comfort and value of your home or business.

Advantages of Pergolas

  • Aesthetic, modern appearance in any environment
  • Protection from rain, sun, and wind, usable all year round
  • Movable roof, shading as needed
  • Expandable with side walls, LED lighting, heating
  • Increases the value and comfort of the property
  • Easy to clean, minimal maintenance
