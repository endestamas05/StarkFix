Venetian Blinds

Venetian blinds represent a contemporary slatted exterior shading system, offering precise control of light and heat, even on large glass surfaces. The tilt angle of the slats can be adjusted steplessly, allowing optimal regulation of natural light and interior temperature while maintaining the view.
Venetian blinds are available with manual or motorised operation, with rail or cable guidance systems, and in a wide range of colours and slat types.
They are an ideal choice for the faÃ§ades of modern family homes, offices, and public buildings, where both functionality and a clean, aesthetic appearance are important considerations.

Advantages of Venetian Blinds

  • Excellent light and heat control, even total blackout
  • Modern, aesthetic appearance for any facade
  • Motor or manual control, smart home integration
  • Durable, weather-resistant aluminum slats
  • Energy saving: cool in summer, warm in winter
  • Many colors, slat shapes (C, S, Z)
  • Ideal shading for family homes, offices, and modern buildings
