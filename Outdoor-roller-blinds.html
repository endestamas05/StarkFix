Outdoor Roller Blinds

Outdoor roller blinds offer effective protection against sunlight, heat and prying eyes. Their modern appearance and versatility make them an ideal choice for family homes, offices, and commercial premises.
This product is available with either manual or motorised operation, and can be combined with insect screens.
It is also available in a variety of colours and designs.

Advantages of Outdoor Roller Blinds

  • Effective heat and sun protection, reduces overheating
  • Reliable protection against the elements
  • Complete blackout and privacy as needed
  • Easy to use, motorized or manual operation
  • Can be combined with insect screens
  • Long lifespan, minimal maintenance
  • Wide range of colors and types, modern design
