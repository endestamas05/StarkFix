Conservatory Shading

Conservatory shading systems have been developed specifically for use in conservatories, glass roofs and large glass surfaces.
They enable precise control of the amount of incoming light, reduce overheating in summer, and create a pleasant climate all year round.
This product is available with either motor or manual operation, and can be purchased in various colours and materials.

Advantages of Conservatory Shading

  • Effective heat protection, reduces overheating
  • Protection against harmful UV radiation
  • Pleasant climate in summer and winter
  • Motor or manual operation
  • Aesthetic appearance, various colors and materials
  • Easy to clean, weather resistant
