Awnings

Awnings are the perfect way to shade terraces, balconies and hospitality venues. They are easy to operate, have a modern appearance and can cover large areas.
They are available with manual or motorised operation and in a wide range of colours and materials, so they can be tailored to any need.

Advantages of Awnings

  • Large shaded area, free space utilization
  • Easy to use, manual or motorized operation
  • Weather-resistant, UV-resistant fabric
  • Modern, aesthetic appearance
  • Wide range of colors and patterns
  • Long lifespan, minimal maintenance
